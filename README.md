## üìä Key Highlights & Insights
- **The Energy Catalyst**: Statistically identified **Access to Electricity** as the single most powerful predictor for rapid gains in life expectancy in emerging markets.
- **Development Decoupling**: Observed the "Plateau Effect" in G7 nations where incremental GDP growth no longer correlates with significant social gains, signaling a shift toward quality-of-life over quantity-of-wealth.
- **Green Transition Audit**: Uncovered early evidence of green decoupling in advanced economies, separating economic activity from carbon debt.
- **The Jobs Gap Warning**: Flagged "Jobless Growth" trends where national GDP expansion failed to trigger labor market stability.
